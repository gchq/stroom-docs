---
title: "Feed"
linkTitle: "Feed"
description: >
  A Feed is a means of organising and categorising data in Stroom.
  A Feed contains multiple _Streams_ of data that have been ingested into Stroom or output by a _Pipeline_.
  Typically a Feed will contain _Streams_ of data that are all from one system and have a common data format.
