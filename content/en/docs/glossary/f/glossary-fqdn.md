---
title: "Fully Qualified Domain Name (FQDN)"
linkTitle: "FQDN"
description: >
  The **F**ully **Q**ualified **D**omain **N**ame (FQDN) is the complete, unambiguous address of a device or service on the internet, specifying all domain levels including the hostname, domain name, and top-level domain.
  For example `server57.some.domain.com`.
